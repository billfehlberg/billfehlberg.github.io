Guess The Fish

How to play:

Select the "Let's Play" button"

Depress any letter on keyboard to try to reveal the clue.

If your selection matches your letter will be revealed. If your letter is not part of the clue you lose a turn.

You will be allowed 7 incorrect guesses.

Reveal all letters from the clue word and you win.
